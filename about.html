A Little About Myself

I've always had a passion for film and video productions.  I started my career at Shawnee Mission Northwest High School.  I made videos for a monthly show that would air throughout the school.  I worked on every aspect: acting, writing, filming, and editing.  I've worked with Final Cut Pro and Adobe Premiere Pro.  I also know how to use Photoshop and Illustrator.  I took these skills to William Jewell College where I am currently studying communications, interactive digital media, and leadership.  I've made videos for the volleyball team, track team, a sorority, a freshmen dorm, and a freshman orientation video for the Student Life office.  

I plan to take this passion and turn it into a career in the future.  I'm eager to learn more about this career and challenge myself at the same time.  I've been doing this for six years now and have only scratched the surface.
